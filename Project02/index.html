HI I’M JON

I’m a programmer, technologist and a lover of all things digital. I live on the south coast of England and run my own technology company Digital Prompt.

Ever since I can remember I have always been interested in solving problems and I’ve always had a slightly odd obsession for simplicity. When I first started in the industry it was this notion of creating the most elegant and simplest solution possible that inspired me to become better at my craft and it’s still the reason why I love what I do.v

In my downtime I’m a lover of books, lifting weights, gigs, festivals and a cheeky drink for Christmas. I love old Kung-fu movies and I’m addicted to ice lollies!
